Body Building

Workout plan recommended for body building:

  • Squats - 20 x 4 reps (Strength Training: Intermediate)
  • Tricep dips - 15 x 4 reps (Strength Training: Advanced)
  • Hanumanasana - 3 minutes x 4 reps (Yoga: Advanced)
  • Push-ups - 15 x 4 reps (Strength Training: Advanced)
  • Pull-ups - 15 x 4 reps (Strength Training: Intermediate)

Follow the given regime for at least 2 months along with a proper diet to achieve the desired result!
