LATERAL SHUFFLES

Steps to follow:

  1. Start standing with feet hip distance apart and sink into a deep knee bend. Bring both hands in front of the chest in guard position.
  2. Start with right foot moving right and left foot following. Shuffle right for four right-left steps, then move left for four left-right steps. Continue shuffling right left for the desired amount of time.
  3. Keep your core engaged the whole time and make sure your chest stays lifted. This will help you maintain proper form.

Video reference:

Benefits of doing Lateral Shuffles:

  1. Improves agility.
  2. Boosts metabolism.
  3. Increases the ability of an athelete to move quickly in every direction

JUMPING JACKS

Steps to follow:

  1. Stand upright with your legs together, arms at your sides.
  2. Bend your knees slightly, and jump into the air.
  3. As you jump, spread your legs to be about shoulder-width apart. Stretch your arms out and over your head.
  4. Jump back to starting position.
  5. Repeat.

Video reference:

Benefits of doing Jumping Jacks:

  1. Increases flexibility.
  2. Relieves stress.
  3. Strengthens glutes and hamstrings.

SQUAT TO FRONT KICK

Steps to follow:

  1. Stand with the feet hip-width apart and arms at the sides.
  2. Bend at the knees to squat down.
  3. Return to standing and extend one leg to perform a front kick.
  4. Repeat the kick on the other side.

Video reference:

Benefits of doing Squat to Front Kick:

  1. Improves balance
  2. Leads to better agility.
  3. Works the core muscles.

TRUNK ROTATION

Steps to follow:

  1. Stand with a heavy object at chest height with elbows to the sides.
  2. Twist from the torso, turning to one side, then the other.
  3. A person can also hold the arms at shoulder height and twist side to side.

Video reference:

Benefits of doing Trunk Rotation

  1. Works on abdominal muscles.
  2. Enhances the cardiovascular system.
  3. Flexibility is increased.

MOUNTAIN CLIMBERS

Steps to follow:

  1. Start in a pushup position with the right leg extended backward and the left leg near the chest with the toes on the ground.
  2. Keeping the hands on the ground and hips level, quickly switch the positions of the legs.
  3. Continue to alternate the legs.

Video reference:

Benefits of doing Mountain Climbers:

  1. Builds cardio endurance.
  2. Stabilises core.
  3. Reduces fat in the abdominal area.

BURPEES

Steps to follow:

  1. Start with the body in a pushup position.
  2. Push off the balls of the feet to bring the knees into the chest and land in a squat.
  3. Jump out of the squat, raising the hands in the air before landing softly back into the squat.
  4. Place the hands back on the floor underneath the shoulders.
  5. Spring the legs back to return to the starting position.

Video reference:

Benefits of doing Mountain Climbers:

  1. Improved blooad flow.
  2. Stronger heart and lungs.
  3. Lower blood pressure.
