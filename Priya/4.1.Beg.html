Beginner Friendly

Exercises recommended for a beginner:

  • Vajrasana - 3 minutes x 2 reps (Yoga: Beginner)
  • Basic Crunches - 15 x 2 reps (Strength Training: Beginner)
  • Jumping Jacks - 20 x 2 reps (Cardio: Beginner)
  • Naukasana - 5 minutes x 1 rep (Toga: Beginner)
  • Lateral Shuffles - 20 x 2 reps (Cardio: Beginner)

Follow the given regime for at least 2-3 weeks along with a proper diet to get into the zone of regular workout!
