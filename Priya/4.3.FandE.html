Flexibility and Endurance

Workout plan recommended to increase flexibility and endurance:

  • Lunges - 20 x 3 reps (Strength Training: Beginner)
  • Squat to front kick - 10 x 3 reps (Cardio: Intyermediate)
  • Halasana - 2 minutes x 4 reps (Yoga: Intermediate)
  • Burpees - 15 x 2 reps (Cardio: Advanced)
  • Kakasana - 2 minutes x 4 reps (Yoga: Advanced)

Follow the given regime for at least 2 months along with a proper diet to achieve the desired result!
